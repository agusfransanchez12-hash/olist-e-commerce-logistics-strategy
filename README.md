## Executive Summary
This project analyzes **Olist’s Brazilian E-Commerce public dataset** to identify logistics bottlenecks, quantify financial exposure due to SLA delays, and provide an actionable strategy to optimize revenue and customer satisfaction.

* **Financial Impact:** **$1.07M in GMV at risk** due to delayed orders (~8.1% of platform total GMV).
* **Customer Satisfaction:** Logistics delays trigger a **rating drop from 4.21★ to 2.55★**, multiplying 1-star reviews by **5.8x**.
* **Operational Overhead:** WISMO (Where Is My Order?) support tickets surge by **3x to 5x**.

## 🎯 Strategic Recommendations & Target ROI

1. **Pillar 1 | Logistics (3PL Regional Partnerships):** Partner with local carriers in high-delay states (**AL, MA, SE**) to cut transit times by **15%–20%**.  
   * **Target ROI:** Protects **+$250K+ in regional GMV**.

2. **Pillar 2 | Operations (Seller Scorecards & Penalties):** Implement automated dispatch scorecards to penalize chronic late-shipping sellers.  
   * **Target ROI:** Reduces WISMO tickets by **30%** & restores average rating to **>4.0★**.

3. **Pillar 3 | Customer Experience (Dynamic Delivery Estimates):** Calculate checkout delivery dates dynamically based on real-time regional performance.  
   * **Target ROI:** Prevents **1-star reviews** caused by unrealistic delivery promises.
